Hollister Co.

Jeans for Girls

At Hollister, we make fit happen. We’re focused on providing the most comfortable fits and fabrics so that you can feel good just being you in your jeans. From the latest, trending styles to simple, clean and classic, we’re creating jeans you’ll want to live in. Speaking of trending, you should check out our collection of mom jeans – they’re not like a regular mom jean, they’re a cool mom jean. Designed in a comfortable, relaxed fit that’s slim through the thigh and loose through the calf, you’ll definitely want more than one pair. Similar to the mom jean, but with a slightly wider leg, the straight jean is another loose, comfortable fit you’ll be seeing everywhere this season. But if a fitted, curve-hugging jean is more in your comfort zone, you’ll love our jeggings and girls super skinny jeans. Made with stretch, each pair fits to your curves and shows off your shape. And our ultra high-rise and high-rise jeans hit higher on the waist for an even more flattering, waist-defining fit. So flattering, you’ll probably want more than one pair. Lucky for you, we have so. many. styles. Check out our range of washes, from super light to dark and just about everything in between. You want it, we got it. Stock your closet with every color. And don’t forget about the hem. Available in full-length and crop, find all your favorite hem treatments within our collection. Like frayed hems, cuffed hems, let-down hems and more. We have clean hems, too, for when you want to keep it classic. But if clean and classic isn’t your thing, check out our collection of ripped jeans for girls for the ultimate lived-in, loved-hard look, complete with vintage washes, fading and fraying. Style with an off-the-shoulder top, comfy tee or basically, anything. In Hollister jeans, your outfitting options are endless.
