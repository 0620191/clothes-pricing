T-Shirts & Tanks for Girls

Looking for the ultimate outfit essentials? Hollister’s girls t-shirts and tanks, otherwise known as the necessary ingredients for every girl’s wardrobe. Our tees and tanks are super soft, totally on trend and pair well with pretty much everything in your closet. Layer them up or wear them on their own. Tuck them in or leave them hanging. From classic silhouettes to stand-out stunners (in an endless variety of colors and prints), these stylish staples are anything but basic and sure to suit every personality.

Are you a jeans and t-shirt kind of girl? We love our easy pocket t-shirts and must have v-necks. Wear them with hi-rise jean short shorts or your very favorite jeans for that no-brainer, laid-back look. New details like curved hems and washed dye details make super cool updates. And you can always rep your true Hollister style with a new short sleeve logo tunic tee. Do you love to layer? We recommend the scoop neck tank or the easy woven cami. Try pairing with a light jacket or sweater for outfitting made easy. And for the girl that craves some edge, we try t-shirts with killer cutout details. Try a cold-shoulder tee or a sweet little crop, or one of our new backless tanks. Paired with a skirt, it’s a look that’s a little more elevated, but just as effortless and super cute, in our humble opinion.

Versatile and effortless, Hollister t-shirts and tanks make perfect closet companions all year round. And they’re available in slim as well as oversized fits, so whatever your mood, whatever the occasion, you’re covered in style and comfort. Dress them up. Dress them down. The options are endless. If you love our basic Hollister tees, also check out graphic t-shirts for girls, and our totally adorable blouses. And if you’re looking for a totally together but chill look check out our girls button up polo shirts in patterns and solids.
