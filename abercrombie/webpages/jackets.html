Lightweight Jackets for Women

If you’re looking for a lightweight jacket, look no further. Abercrombie & Fitch has an amazing selection of womens jackets and outerwear, including some essential light layers to take you from season to season-or simply to polish off your perfect outfit. A shirt jacket is an easy add-on to any pair of womens jeans or skirts. And our Twill Shirt Jacket has a military feel that’s always on trend, and with colors like khaki, olive, and more, it goes with everything in your closet. And our Classic Jean jacket is a total no-brainer. It’s the easiest thing to just grab and go, but still look like you put some thought into your amazing look. Over one of our womens dresses with a pair of sneakers, it’s pretty, casual, and cool. But with a pair of skinny jeans, it’s an eternally hip look that’s almost too easy. We have embellished details like embroidered patchwork, but if you just want to keep it classic, you can’t go wrong with our denim and twill jackets.

From the softest twill fabric to light windbreaker styles, we have everything to keep you warm in a mild chill, dry when it rains, and always on point when it comes to flawless style. Abercrombie & Fitch womens jackets come in sizes XS to XL, so your perfect size is easy to find. For slightly more warmth, try one of our womens bomber jackets or parkas; two more options that keep you cozy but never skimp on style. And remember, our cardigan sweaters make a great alternative to any lightweight layer requirements.
